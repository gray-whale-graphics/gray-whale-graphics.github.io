DeathSong (adult)

Large Mystery Class

Although deceptively beautiful in appearance and melody, the Death Song has a lethal quality that is reminiscent of the Black Widow. This dragon vocalizes its beautiful and hypnotic siren call in order to lure other dragons, which it then targets as prey. However, Death Songs can be friendly towards humans and other dragons they trust, but are only trainable if you sing a song that they enjoy.
