Nightfury (baby)

Medium Strike Class

The Night Fury was once considered to be one of the most mysterious and fearsome species of dragons. They have the ability to "dive bomb," meaning that they can plummet from great heights, gaining speed as they continue downwards, then shoot a "plasma blast" at their target. Night Furies, like Toothless, are often characterized as smart, stealthy, and intelligent. They are also known for their loyalty and protective nature when it comes to those they care about. While they can be aloof and secretive initially, they are also playful and curious, especially when it comes to new things they observe.
