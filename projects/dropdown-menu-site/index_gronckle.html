Gronckle (Baby)

Medium Boulder Class

They are generally described as fussy, stubborn, and slothful but with a very sweet and affectionate side towards their rider. They are known for their heavy armor, slow movement, and ability to swallow rocks and fire them as molten lava. One of the toughest dragons in our world is the Gronckle. Gronckles have gigantic heads, short bodies, and round tails. Gronckles are lazy, and spend most of their time sleeping in communal heaps. They are slow in the air, but make up for this with their maneuverability. A Gronckle can fly backwards, sideways, and even hover. They have been known to fall asleep while flying, waking only when they crash into the ocean or the side of a mountain.
