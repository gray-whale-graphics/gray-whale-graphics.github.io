Zippleback (baby)

Large Mystery Class

A zippleback is characterized by two separate minds, each with a distinct personality, residing in its two heads. While social and willing to repay debts, their heads frequently squabble and argue. Unlike other dragons, the zippleback does not breathe fire. Instead, they make explosions! One head breathes green, flammable gas, while the other uses an electrical spark to ignite it, creating the explosions.

The Zippleback is one of the largest dragons you may ever encounter. Like most dragons, Zipplebacks can fly, but their wings are small and they spend most of their time on the ground. Zipplebacks are solitary, preferring to hunt alone.
